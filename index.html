Design pillars

Visual discipline

High-contrast typography
Snow-white headings and white body text on black chrome surfaces.
Contained emphasis
Key actions and identity signals are boxed, framed, and isolated rather than scattered.
One disciplined accent
Signal red is reserved for emphasis, risk, and navigation energy rather than sprayed everywhere.
Reduced-motion compliance
All motion degrades cleanly when users prefer reduced motion.
