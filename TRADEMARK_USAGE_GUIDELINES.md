# Trademark and Naming Guidelines

These guidelines govern use of repository names, titles, logos, and presentation identity.

## Protected identifiers
Unless separately authorized in writing, do not present your own materials as:
- KRITERION
- GPT5.4-AUDIT-HARDENING-PROTOCOL-2026
- any confusingly similar title implying official status or origin

## Permitted nominative use
You may accurately refer to the repository by name when:
- citing it,
- reviewing it,
- linking to it,
- describing compatibility,
provided you do not imply endorsement or authorship.

## Prohibited use
You may not:
- rebrand your product or consulting service with these names;
- use the repository logo as your own mark;
- imply certification, approval, partnership, or authorship by the repository owner.
